FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tree services.

Tree removal costs vary based on the size, location, and condition of the tree. Small trees may start around $300, while large or hazardous trees can range from $1,000 to $3,000+. We provide free on-site estimates so you know the exact cost before any work begins.

Yes, we offer emergency tree services for storm damage, fallen trees, and other urgent situations. Our crew can typically respond quickly in the greater Clinton, LA area.

Absolutely. Big Bass Tree Services is fully licensed and carries comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

We serve Clinton, LA and the surrounding parishes including East Feliciana, West Feliciana, East Baton Rouge, St. Helena, and Livingston. Contact us if you're outside these areas — we may still be able to help.

Signs that a tree may need removal include large dead branches, trunk decay or cavities, a leaning trunk, root damage, fungal growth at the base, or significant storm damage. Our certified arborists can assess your tree and recommend the best course of action.

In many cases, homeowner's insurance will cover tree removal if the tree fell due to a storm or covered event and damaged a structure. We can work with your insurance company and provide the documentation needed for your claim.
